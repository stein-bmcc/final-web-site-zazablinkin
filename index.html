Audacity/ What it is

Audacity is an open-source audio production software that allows users to record, edit, and enhance audio. I use it to record sound, then edit by cutting unwanted parts and moving sections around. I apply effects like equalization, compression, reverb, and pitch adjustments to improve audio quality. Audacity also lets me layer multiple tracks, mixing vocals, music, and sound effects seamlessly. Once the editing is complete, I export the final product in various formats for sharing or uploading. It’s a versatile tool that helps bring creative audio projects to life.
